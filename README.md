# Publicis-Pulse-Survey

Description - This project is about creating a Power BI dashboard that shows feedback received from clients through a survey called Publicis Pulse Survey. The survey is sent twice a year to understand how happy clients are with Publicis agencies.

1. Project Overview / Problem Statement

“The project was called the Publicis Pulse Engagement Survey Dashboard. The main objective was to analyze client feedback collected through surveys across different Publicis agencies globally. The business wanted a centralized dashboard to monitor client satisfaction, compare agency performance, identify low-performing regions, and support leadership in making data-driven decisions.”

2. Your Role and Contribution

“I worked as a Business Intelligence Analyst on this project. My responsibilities included data extraction and validation using SQL, data cleaning and transformation using Power Query, creating data models, developing interactive Power BI dashboards, writing DAX measures for KPIs, and implementing row-level security. I also supported business users with enhancements, ad-hoc analysis, and dashboard validation.”

3. Data Sources and Tools

“The data was primarily sourced from survey datasets and SQL databases. SQL Server was used for data extraction and validation. Power BI was used for dashboard development and visualization. Power Query was used for ETL and data transformation, while DAX was used for KPI calculations and dynamic reporting. Excel export functionality was also provided for business users.”

4. Process / Methodology

“First, data was collected and validated from multiple sources. Then, I cleaned and transformed the data using Power Query. After that, I created a star schema data model with fact and dimension tables to optimize performance and filtering. I developed DAX measures for KPIs and comparisons, designed interactive dashboard pages, implemented drill-down functionality and row-level security, and finally performed UAT and validation before deployment.”

5. Key Features of the Solution

“•Enabled leadership to quickly view overall client satisfaction for the current wave through NPS and Star Ratings with clear color-coded
indicators.
•Helped business users compare satisfaction wave-over-wave, allowing them to track whether service quality improved or declined
between April and September.
•Allowed regional and country leaders to identify underperforming geographies and take targeted corrective actions.
•Highlighted which agencies were performing well and which required immediate attention, supporting decision-making during
performance reviews.
•Provided visibility into survey participation levels by showing completed vs sent counts, helping teams follow up with low-response
markets.
•Revealed patterns in scoring behavior using distribution charts, helping analysts understand whether dissatisfaction was widespread
or driven by a few respondents.
•Showed how satisfaction varies by client tenure, seniority, and decision-maker level, helping business teams focus on influential or
long-term client relationships.
•Supported comparison of service quality across People, Delivery, Services, and Strategy to identify which service areas consistently
scored low.
•Provided a consistent, drill-down view from region → sub-region → country → agency → client, improving transparency across the
global network.”

6. Impact and Results

“The dashboard helped leadership quickly identify underperforming agencies and regions, monitor client satisfaction trends, and take corrective actions. It improved reporting efficiency, reduced manual analysis effort, and provided better visibility into client feedback globally. The project also supported faster decision-making through centralized and interactive reporting.”

7. Key Learnings

“Through this project, I gained strong hands-on experience in Power BI dashboard development, DAX calculations, data modeling, and handling large survey datasets. I also improved my understanding of business reporting requirements, stakeholder communication, performance optimization, and implementing secure reporting solutions using row-level security.”

8. Future Enhancements / Monitoring Support

“Post-deployment, I supported dashboard monitoring, handled enhancement requests, and performed data validation during new releases. Future enhancements could include automated alerts for low satisfaction scores, predictive analytics for client churn risk, and integrating additional business metrics for deeper analysis.”
